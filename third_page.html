Student Life

SPPS is motivated by the belief that nurturing students to develop a clear sense of responsibility will develop global citizens who transform the world for the better, to achieve overall harmony. Understanding our nature and the need for interpersonal relationships, we strongly encourage students to participate in as many community service activities as possible to increase their awareness of social responsibility.


School-Sponsored Community Service Activities


"Shoes of Hope" helping child labors in developing countries

"Daoom English Library Tutoring" for kids from low-income families

Support for students life: Homeroom System


The SPPS Homeroom system provides a strong of support to make students feel connected and engaged while providing opportunities in leadership. Accomplishing various projects and competitions cooperatively throughout the year, each homeroom builds a unique identity that enhances a stronger sense of cohesion and belongingness to the community.
